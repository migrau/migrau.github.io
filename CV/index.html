Personal statement

Hi, I am Miguel Grau. I am a bionformatic researcher currently working at Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology in Japan. My main task consists in analyse Next Generation Sequencing data, especially focused in the evolution aspects related.

I design distinct pipes for a huge variety of ecological questions. These tasks include the use of illumina/pacbio data to perform new assemblies, transcriptome analysis/annotation, SNPs population genomics studies, phylogenetics, analysis of positive selection genes, mitochondrial genomes and proteins analysis across distinct related species, among others.

The pipes make use of multiple software tools, as well as our own libraries and scripts, using mainly (and not only) Python and R.
